Emergency Fitting

We understand that urgent tyre replacement can be inconvenient and impractical. Our emergency mobile fitting service offers a hassle-free solution to your urgent tyre needs, wherever your vehicle may be. Our skilled professionals provide new tyre installation, new valves, wheel balancing, and disposal of old tyres for a complete service.

Tyre repair

Our skilled technicians can determine if a tyre repair is possible for your flat tyre. Factors such as tyre size, speed rating, load rating, and damage extent are all taken into consideration, following British Standard BS AU 159. Damage to the side walls or shoulders is deemed unsafe, but a small split or cut to the central tread area can often be repaired with an internal plug. Contact us online for tailored advice to your vehicle needs.

Wheel balance check

Proper wheel balance is crucial for your vehicle's performance and safety. Our expert team can identify and resolve any balance issues you may be experiencing, which can cause wobbling or uneven tyre wear. If you are purchasing new tyres from us, our full wheel balance check and rebalancing service is included in the price. Contact us for a comprehensive check of your wheels.
